SYS.03 / How I work

Senior habits, boring reliability.

  • Map before building.

    Every engagement starts with a system map of what actually exists. A mess that's mapped is already half fixed.

  • Dry-run before production.

    Changes rehearse against test records before they touch a live pipeline or a spending campaign.

  • Documented so it survives me.

    SOPs, walkthrough videos, credentials in your name. When we're done, you don't need me to keep the lights on.

  • Async-first.

    Loom updates over meetings — you see progress without booking a call. Live calls when they earn their slot.

  • Timezone as a feature.

    Philippines base means US afternoons and AU mornings live, and your systems watched while you sleep.
