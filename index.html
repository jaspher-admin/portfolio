FAQ / Before you ask

Straight answers.

How does pricing work?

Fixed-scope quotes, priced from the system map — you approve the number before anything gets built. Ongoing Systems Lead engagements run on a monthly retainer. No hourly meters running in the background.

What actually happens on the free audit call?

You share your screen, we walk through your stack live, and I point at things. You leave with three prioritized fixes and a build-vs-don't answer. Some people ship the fixes themselves and never hire me — that's a fine outcome.

Do I own everything you build?

Yes. Accounts, automations, code, and documentation all live in your name and your infrastructure. When we're done, you don't need me to keep the lights on.

How do time zones work?

I'm in the Philippines, which overlaps US afternoons and AU mornings for live calls. Everything else runs async with Loom updates — most clients find they get more visibility this way, not less.

How long does a typical build take?

Most fixed-scope builds ship in one to three weeks of weekly sprints. Dashboards like ARIA take longer; single-workflow fixes take days. The proposal gives you a real date, not a range this vague.

My stack is a genuine mess. Is that a problem?

That's the normal starting point — nobody books an audit because everything works. Step one is always a map of what exists, and a mess that's mapped is already half fixed.
